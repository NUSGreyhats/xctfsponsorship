What is X-CTF?



“X-CTF” is the abbreviation for “Cross Varsity - Capture The Flag” competition. It is a cyber security competition organized annually in a controlled environment where teams from all tertiary institutes are welcomed to participate in. The competition will be held in the duration of eight hours and each team will be given the same set of challenges to crack in order to retrieve a “flag”. The flag will be converted to points upon submission to the panel and the team with the highest amount of points will emerge as champion.

NUS Greyhats is proud to organise X-CTF 2016 where student teams from various tertiary institutes will compete to solve challenges which are set by an independent body.
